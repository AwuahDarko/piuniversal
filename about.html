About Us

Welcome to our website

Pi Universal (PiU)

Pi Universal is a Ghanaian own company whose main aim is to lead Technology innovations and inventions world wide. Its founder and president is Darko Awuah Jackson - Technology enthusiat who believes the best way for the development of Ghana and Africa is to invest more resources (human and capital) in technology.

Pi Universal is the parent company of Pi Innovative Solutions - considered as its software department and Pi Electron - considered as it electronics department.

Pi Innovative Solutions (PiIS)

Though registered as a seperate business is the department under Pi Universal whose main concern is to develop software solutions to most of the problems in Ghana, Africa and the World at large.

Pi Electrons (PiE)

This the electronics department of Pi Universal whose main concern is to develop hardware solution solutions to solve problems in Ghana, Africa and the world at large. It collaborate with the software department (PiIS) to develop cutting edge technologies.
