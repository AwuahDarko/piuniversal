Our Services

Pi Innovative Solutions Ghana is the developer of SkyScraper(Desktop) and Castle(mobile) softwares. It provides an easy way to monitor staff attendance, do payroll, file PAYE taxes based of current Ghana Revenue Authority (GRA) tax rates.

Attendance Monitoring

Monitor staff presence at work place with our biometric (finger print) system or use our GPS System to track employees at their various postings.

Payroll

With our softwares you can do the complex computations involved in monthly payroll such as social security deductions, taxation, bonuses, benefits/allowances, etc with just a click of a button.

Pay slip and Timesheets

Send employee payslip and time sheets directly to the email of your staff.

PAYE Tax

Our system is designed to generate all tax forms based on the Ghana Revenue Authority format for both monthly and annual deductions schedules, all you have to do is print/download and submit.

Pi Innovative Solutions is the developer of the Staff Management / Payroll / Attendance / Tax App in Ghana: SkyScraper (desktop) and Castle (mobile).
